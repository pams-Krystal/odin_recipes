Fried Plantain

How to fry plantain - Fried plantain.

This Fried plantain recipe is straightforward and quick. Crisp outside and fluffy inside, plantain is perfect as sides to rice dishes and beans dishes.

Ingredients

  • Bunch of Ripe Plantain
  • Salt
  • Vegetable cooking oil

Steps

  1. Peel and slice plantains.
  2. Add the vegetable cooking oil in a pan and put on medium heat. When oil is heated, arrange the already sliced plantains in the oil.
  3. Fry for about 5 minutes on medium heat or until golden brown, then flip over the plantain to fry the other side.
  4. Transfer the plantain from the heat to a paper-towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  5. Season with salt, if desired and serve.
